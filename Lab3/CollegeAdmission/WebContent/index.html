IBM Institute of Technology : Admission 2017

Invites application to admission for the year 2017-2018 for the BE Courses commencing in July/August. Admission is based on the merit of JEE. All applicants are therefore required to appear in JEE.

Eligibility

The eligibility criteria for admission is given in detail in the Prospectus which is available in February every year. Brief outline is as below :- Candidate Should have passed, or appeared for 10+2 exam, at the time of applying for admission . Minimum acceptable mark in PCM are 50%. Age of the candidate must be between 16 to 21 years on 01 Jul 2017. Must be an Indian citizen, or a PIO who obtains Indian citizenship before admission.
